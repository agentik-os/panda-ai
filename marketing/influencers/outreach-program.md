## Outreach Strategy

### Phase 1: Research (Week 1)

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Identify 50+ potential influencers
- [ ] Categorize by tier and platform
- [ ] Follow their content for 1 week
- [ ] Note what they care about
- [ ] Find mutual connections (warm intros)

**Tools:**
- Twitter Lists (AI influencers)
- YouTube search (AI tools reviews)
- LinkedIn (tech influencers)
- Newsletter aggregators (tech newsletters)

### Phase 2: Warm-Up (Week 1-2)

**Before Outreach:**
- [ ] Follow them on socials
- [ ] Engage with their content (meaningful comments)
- [ ] Share their content (if genuinely valuable)
- [ ] Build relationship (not transactional)

**Goal:** Be a familiar name before asking for anything.

### Phase 3: Outreach (Week 2-3)

**Outreach Email Template:**

```
Subject: Agentik OS - Self-hosted AI agent platform (would love your take)

Hey [Name],

I'm [Your Name], creator of Agentik OS - a new self-hosted AI agent platform with multi-model routing.

I've been following your work on [specific content they created], especially [specific point that resonated]. Your perspective on [topic] really influenced how we built [feature].

**Why I'm Reaching Out:**

We just launched Agentik OS, and I think your audience would find it interesting because:
1. [Reason specific to their audience]
2. [Feature that aligns with their content]
3. [Problem it solves they've mentioned]

**What It Is:**
- Self-hosted AI agent platform
- Multi-model routing (Claude, GPT, Gemini, Ollama)
- Transparent cost tracking
- Open source (Apache 2.0)
- Built to compete with OpenClaw (191K stars)

**No Ask - Just Sharing:**
I'm not asking you to promote it (unless you genuinely like it!). I'd just love your honest take as someone who deeply understands [their niche].

GitHub: https://github.com/agentik-os/agentik-os
Docs: https://docs.agentik.dev
Demo: [Loom video link]

If you have 10 minutes to try it, I'd be grateful for any feedback (public or private).

Thanks for all your content - it's been invaluable!

Best,
[Your Name]

P.S. Happy to give you early access to [upcoming feature] if you're interested.
```

### Phase 4: Follow-Up (Week 3-4)

**If They Respond Positively:**
```
Hey [Name],

Thanks so much for trying Agentik OS! üôè

[Address their specific feedback]

If you'd like to share it with your audience, here's what works well:
- [Talking point 1]
- [Talking point 2]
- [Unique angle for their audience]

No pressure at all - appreciate your time either way!

Want early access to [feature]? Happy to give you a sneak peek.
```

**If No Response (Wait 1 Week):**
```
Hey [Name],

Quick follow-up on my previous email about Agentik OS.

Totally understand if you're busy! Just wanted to share that we hit [milestone] and [new feature] just launched.

Still would love your take if you have time, but no worries if not.

[Brief reminder of what it is]

Thanks!
```

**If Still No Response:**
- Don't push further (respect their time)
- Keep engaging with their content
- Maybe they'll discover it organically
