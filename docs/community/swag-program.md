## How to Earn Swag

### Tier 1: First Contributor (Starter Pack)
**Requirements:**
- 1 merged PR (any type)
- OR 3 quality issue reports
- OR 10 helpful Discord/discussion responses

**Swag:**
- Sticker pack (5 stickers)
- Digital contributor badge

**How to claim:**
1. Your first PR gets merged
2. Automatic invite email sent within 48 hours
3. Fill out shipping form
4. Receive swag in 2-4 weeks

### Tier 2: Active Contributor (T-Shirt + Stickers)
**Requirements:**
- 5 merged PRs
- OR 20+ support interactions
- OR Significant documentation contribution (10+ pages)

**Swag:**
- Contributor edition t-shirt
- Premium sticker pack (15 stickers)
- Custom GitHub profile badge

**How to claim:**
1. Reach tier requirement
2. Automatic notification
3. Choose size and design
4. Ships within 2-4 weeks

### Tier 3: Major Contributor (Hoodie + Full Pack)
**Requirements:**
- 10+ merged PRs
- OR Built popular marketplace skill (100+ installs)
- OR Created comprehensive tutorial series
- OR Active maintainer role

**Swag:**
- Premium hoodie (embroidered logo)
- Full sticker collection
- Enamel pin set
- Challenge coin

**How to claim:**
1. Auto-notified when eligible
2. Select premium options
3. Priority shipping (1-2 weeks)

### Tier 4: Core Team / Exceptional Contributor
**Requirements:**
- Core team member
- OR 50+ merged PRs
- OR Exceptional one-time contribution (major feature, critical security fix)
- OR Won hackathon grand prize

**Swag:**
- Everything from Tier 3
- Signed items by core team
- Limited edition items
- Custom contributor card
- Lifetime swag subscription (new items sent quarterly)

## Swag Quality Standards

We believe in quality over quantity:
- **T-shirts**: Premium cotton, eco-friendly inks
- **Hoodies**: Heavyweight fleece, embroidered (not printed)
- **Stickers**: Die-cut vinyl, weather-resistant
- **Pins**: Soft enamel with rubber backing
- **Coins**: Metal alloy with antique finish

All items produced ethically with sustainable materials where possible.

## FAQs

### Can I request specific items?
Tier 1-2: No, standardized packs
Tier 3-4: Yes, choose from available options

### Can I order extra swag for friends?
Swag is for contributors only, but you can refer friends to contribute!

### I moved, can I update my address?
Email: swag@agentik-os.com with new address

### Can I decline swag and donate to the project instead?
Yes! We appreciate the gesture. Donate via [GitHub Sponsors](https://github.com/sponsors/agentik-os).

### What if swag doesn't fit?
Email us within 30 days, we'll send replacement size.

### Can companies/sponsors get custom swag?
Yes! Contact: partnerships@agentik-os.com for bulk/custom orders.
