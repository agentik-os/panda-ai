## Prize Criteria Breakdown

### Innovation (30%)
- How novel is the idea?
- Has this been done before?
- Is it a creative solution?
- Does it push boundaries?

**Scoring:**
- 9-10: Completely novel approach
- 7-8: Innovative twist on existing idea
- 5-6: Solid implementation, some novelty
- 3-4: Mostly derivative work
- 1-2: Not innovative

### Execution (25%)
- How well is it built?
- Is the code quality high?
- Does it work reliably?
- Is it production-ready?

**Scoring:**
- 9-10: Production-ready, excellent quality
- 7-8: Mostly polished, few bugs
- 5-6: Works but rough edges
- 3-4: Buggy or incomplete
- 1-2: Barely functional

### Impact (25%)
- What problem does it solve?
- Who benefits from it?
- How many people could use it?
- Is it addressing a real need?

**Scoring:**
- 9-10: Solves major problem for many people
- 7-8: Useful for specific audience
- 5-6: Nice-to-have improvement
- 3-4: Marginal utility
- 1-2: No clear use case

### Technical Merit (20%)
- How complex is it?
- Does it use advanced techniques?
- Is the architecture sound?
- Did it require deep expertise?

**Scoring:**
- 9-10: Highly complex, impressive tech
- 7-8: Solid technical implementation
- 5-6: Standard complexity
- 3-4: Basic technical skills
- 1-2: Minimal technical challenge

## FAQ

### Can I win multiple categories?
Yes! One project can win multiple categories and receive all prizes.

### Can I donate my prize?
Yes, you can donate to charity or Agentik OS project.

### What if I'm a minor (under 18)?
Parent/guardian must accept prize on your behalf.

### What if I'm in a sanctioned country?
Unfortunately, we cannot distribute prizes to sanctioned countries due to legal restrictions.

### Can I defer my prize?
No, prizes must be claimed within 30 days or forfeited.

### What if PayPal isn't available in my country?
We'll work with you to find alternative payment method.

### Do I have to pay to enter?
No! Hackathon is 100% free to enter.

### Can prize amounts change?
Prize amounts are fixed once hackathon is announced and cannot be changed.
